What is ibericode?

ibericode BV is the umbrella company for projects undertaken by Danny van Kooten and friends.

We were founded in 2010 and have been busy developing, supporting and maintaining WordPress products since then.

Our plugins have been downloaded well over 20 million times and are in active use on well over 2 million sites.

Most likely, you have even used some of our software already!

What we work on

  • MC4WP: Mailchimp for WordPress, a plugin that allows you to add a multitude of sign-up methods for Mailchimp to your WordPress site.
  • Koko Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics plugin for WordPress that does not use any external services.
  • Boxzilla, a WordPress plugin to show pop-up or slide-in boxes.
  • HTML Forms, a WordPress plugin to build multi-purpose forms.
  • Fathom Analytics, an open-source alternative to Google Analytics. (stepped down)
