What is ibericode?

ibericode BV is the umbrella company for projects undertaken by Danny van Kooten and friends.

We were founded in 2010 and have been busy building and supporting a few marketing related WordPress products since then.

Our plugins have been downloaded well over 20 million times and are in active use on more than 2 million sites.

More than likely, you have used some of our software already.

Our products

  • MailChimp for WordPress, a WordPress plugin that allows you to add a multitude of sign-up methods to your site.
  • Boxzilla, a WordPress plugin to show pop-up or slide-in boxes.
  • HTML Forms, a WordPress plugin to build multi-purpose forms.
  • Fathom Analytics, an open-source alternative to Google Analytics.
